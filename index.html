Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

The ceremony will be held at RAVAL, Piazza del Papa, and the reception will follow at Posatora. Both locations are in Ancona, Italy.

We recommend the Grand Hotel Ancona or Villa Serena for accommodation. Both are located within 15 minutes of our venue and offer special rates for our wedding guests.

The closest airport is Ancona Falconara Airport (AOI). From there, you can rent a car or take a taxi to the venue. Complimentary parking will be available at both the ceremony and reception venues.

The ceremony starts at 4:00 PM. We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes early to find seating and get settled.

Our wedding is formal attire. Suits and ties for men, cocktail or formal dresses for women. The ceremony will be held outdoors, so please consider comfortable shoes for garden terrain.
